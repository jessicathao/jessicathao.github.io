About Me

I'm Le Thi Thach Thao (Jessica), a Ph.D. student in Linguistics at National Taiwan University. My research focuses on sociolinguistics, multilingualism, and AI literacy.

With a background in TESOL (4.0 GPA from NKUST) and years of teaching English to students of all ages, I'm passionate about sharing knowledge and helping others succeed in their academic journeys.

I've presented at international conferences including TESOL, JALT, and Linguapax Asia, and my work has been published in academic proceedings and journals.
