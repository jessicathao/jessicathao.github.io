MY PROFILE

Hi, I'm Thach Thao! 👋

A Vietnamese researcher, English educator, and PhD student passionate about linguistics, multilingualism, and the intersection of AI and language education.

Le Thi Thach Thao

PHD STUDENT · NTU LINGUISTICS

Originally from Vietnam, I've been living and studying in Taiwan since 2019. I completed my Master's in TESOL and Applied Linguistics at NKUST (4.0 GPA, Excellence ranking) and am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Linguistics at National Taiwan University under Professor Chia-Rung Lu, researching sociolinguistics, multilingualism, and AI literacy.

✉️ Email 💼 LinkedIn 🔬 ResearchGate 🎓 Google Scholar 💻 GitHub

🔭Research Interests

  • Sociolinguistics & Multilingualism: Understanding how language shapes identity and community, especially among immigrant families
  • AI & Language Education: Exploring how AI tools like ChatGPT and LLMs can transform English learning
  • Computational Linguistics: Using Python and corpus tools for linguistic analysis
  • Multimodal Metaphor Analysis: Analyzing visual metaphors in climate change advertising
